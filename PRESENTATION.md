### 1. Real-Time Verification
Our platform allows users to submit claims and headlines for immediate analysis. By leveraging a structured methodology, we provide clear, sourced, and objective conclusions.

### 2. Radical Transparency
Every fact-check on TIHTK is accompanied by:
- **Primary Sources**: Direct links to the evidence.
- **Methodology**: A clear explanation of how we reached our conclusion.
- **Confidence Score**: A measurement of the strength of the evidence.

### 3. Source Accountability
We don't just check the news; we check the newsmakers. Our source analysis engine evaluates the history, funding, and bias of various media outlets to provide users with context.

### 4. Educational Empowerment
Our "Learning Paths" guide users through the complexities of logical fallacies, data interpretation, and the psychology behind fake news.
